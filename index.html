WHO WE ARE

Sky Nova is a light alternative rock band creating cinematic songs about people, moments and quiet emotions.

Our music is built like a journey - soft, honest and atmospheric.

We believe that even the smallest story can become a universe.

Our band was at first, as a school band of Gesamtschule Oelde created
Than with a help of Alte Post Oelde, it is in a two-teams of magic-made music developed.
